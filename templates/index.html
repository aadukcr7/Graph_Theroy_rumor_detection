Research Notes

Propagation Trees

Each network models a message cascade from a source node to later reposts.

Temporal Weighting

Fast reposts get larger weights using 1 / log(1 + Δt).

Centrality

Degree, betweenness, and closeness capture how concentrated the diffusion is.

Graph Density and Diameter

Rumors tend to be denser around the source and smaller in diameter than organic chains.

Diffusion Speed

Computed from the mean propagation delay to quantify how quickly the cascade spreads.

Interpretability

The classifier is explainable because every prediction is driven by explicit graph features.
